Common Physiotherapy Services

Choose the concern closest to your need, or select General if you want the therapist to guide you after assessment.

MS

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

Targeted care for back pain, neck stiffness, joint strain, posture issues, and movement restrictions.

  • Back, neck, and shoulder pain care
  • Joint mobility restoration
  • Posture and movement correction
SP

Sports Physiotherapy

Performance-focused rehab for athletes and active adults recovering from sprains, strains, or overuse injuries.

  • Sports injury assessment
  • Strength and return-to-play plans
  • Injury prevention guidance
NP

Neurological Physiotherapy

Structured therapy support for balance, coordination, gait, and functional recovery after neurological conditions.

  • Stroke and neuro rehab support
  • Balance and gait training
  • Functional movement progression
GP

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Safe, supportive physiotherapy for seniors dealing with pain, weakness, balance issues, or recovery after illness.

  • Fall-risk reduction
  • Mobility and balance support
  • Strength for daily activity independence
PP

Paediatric Physiotherapy

Child-friendly movement therapy focusing on posture, motor development, coordination, and functional play.

  • Motor milestone support
  • Coordination and posture care
  • Play-based therapy approach
WH

Women's Health Physiotherapy

Confidential physiotherapy care for women�s mobility, pelvic health, and recovery needs across life stages.

  • Prenatal and postnatal support
  • Pelvic floor guidance
  • Pain and movement recovery plans
VR

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Specialized exercises to improve dizziness, balance disturbances, and movement confidence.

  • Balance retraining
  • Dizziness-focused rehab
  • Functional confidence building
PR

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Guided recovery plans to rebuild mobility, strength, and movement confidence after surgery.

  • Joint and soft-tissue recovery
  • Mobility restoration plans
  • Progressive strength rebuilding
PM

Pain Management Physiotherapy

Movement-based pain management plans that reduce stiffness, improve tolerance, and support long-term relief.

  • Chronic pain support
  • Movement pacing strategies
  • Functional recovery planning
